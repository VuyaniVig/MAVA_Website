About Mava Industries

Established in 2024, Mava Industries is revolutionizing green energy access in South African townships. Our rent-to-own solar solution empowers township landlords to offer reliable energy without upfront costs.

We integrate solar panels, batteries, and smart meters with mobile pay-as-you-go options. This gives landlords visibility and tenants predictable billing — all powered by the sun.

We envision a just, inclusive energy future where communities thrive with climate-friendly solutions. Mava works closely with partners, policy makers, and innovators to reshape township energy one rooftop at a time.

Challenges We Solve

  • Energy Insecurity: Load shedding disrupts daily life in townships.
  • Rising Tariffs: Prepaid electricity is expensive and hard to manage.
  • High Solar Costs: Upfront system costs exclude most township landlords.
  • Unfair Tenant Billing: Landlords face challenges billing fairly without smart meters.

Our Solution: Rent-To-Own Solar

  • Zero upfront cost
  • Affordable monthly payments
  • Smart meters with pay-as-you-go access
  • Ownership after lease term
